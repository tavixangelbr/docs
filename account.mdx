## 2. Account Management & Security

Your security is our priority. We use state-of-the-art authentication systems.

### Login Methods

- **Google Auth:** Fast and secure access with one click.
- **Magic Link:** No passwords! We send a unique code to your email.
- **Traditional Password:** If you prefer the classic method.

### Data Security

All your billing data is processed by **Dodo Payments**, ensuring bank-grade encryption.
