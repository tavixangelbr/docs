## 3. Brand Identity & Voice

The soul of your content lies in your **Brand Identity**.

![Image: Brand Setup Screen](YOUR_IMAGE_URL_HERE)

### Setting up your Profile:

1. **Brand Name:** How the public knows you.
2. **Industry:** Helps the AI understand your niche.
3. **Brand Voice:** Choose between Professional, Friendly, Bold, or Minimalist.
4. **Target Audience:** Who are you writing for?
