## 8. Plans, Credits, and Limits

Understand how Directo's economic engine works.

### Plans Table

- **Starter:** 10 Posts AI/month \+ 100 Credits.
- **Pro:** 30 Posts AI/month \+ 300 Credits.
- **Agency:** Unlimited \+ 1000 Credits.

### Credit Costs:

- **AI Image Generation:** 1 Credit.
- **Manual Post:** 1 Credit.
- **Carousel Generation:** 1 Credit per generated slide.
- **Captions/Hashtags:** 1 Credit.
