## 9. Scheduling & Auto-Publishing

Stop wasting time posting manually on your phone.

### How to Schedule:

1. Finish your design.
2. Click the **Schedule** button.
3. Choose the date and time.
4. Check your **Calendar** to make sure everything is correct.
