## 4. Instagram Connection

To publish automatically, Directo needs a secure bridge to your account.

### Step-by-Step:

1. Your account must be **Business** or **Creator**.
2. It must be linked to a Facebook Page.
3. Click "Connect" and authorize all permissions.

> ⚠️ **Important:** If you do not grant all permissions, scheduling might fail.
