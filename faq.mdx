## 11. Mega FAQ (20\+ Frequently Asked Questions)

**1. Is Directo safe?** Yes, we use official Facebook/Instagram authentication.

**2. Do my credits expire?** Credits are renewed monthly according to your billing cycle.

**3. Can I schedule Stories?** Yes, Directo supports Feed, Reels, and Stories.

**4. How to change the AI language?** In the creation modal, you can choose between Portuguese and English.

**5. Can I use photos from my computer?** Absolutely! Just drag them into the editor or the library.

**6. What happens if I cancel my subscription?** You will keep access until the end of the paid period, but you won't be able to create new posts after expiration.

**7. Does the AI understand my niche's slang?** Yes, if you correctly set your "Brand Voice" in the visual identity settings.

**8. Can I have more than one Instagram connected?** It depends on your plan. The Agency plan allows multiple connections.

**9. Does Directo work on mobile?** The Dashboard is optimized for mobile browsers, but the best editing experience is on Desktop.

**10. How does Directo help with Instagram SEO?** We generate optimized captions and strategic hashtags based on your theme.

**11. Can I generate images of real people?** Our AI generates realistic images, but we always recommend using your own photos for higher authority.

**12. Scheduling failed, what should I do?** Check if your Instagram account is still connected in "Settings". Sometimes Facebook asks to reconnect for security.

**13. Are there refunds?** We offer a satisfaction guarantee. Check our Terms of Use for details.

**14. Can I share my login with my team?** We recommend each user has their own account to avoid editing conflicts.

**15. How do I get more credits without changing plans?** We will soon have standalone credit packs!

**16. Does Directo create videos from scratch?** Currently, we focus on images and carousels, but you can upload your videos and schedule them as Reels.

**17. What is the "Magic Link"?** It's a secure login link we send to your email. You click it and enter your account without needing a password.

**18. Can I edit a post that has already been scheduled?** Yes, just go to the Calendar, open the post, and save the changes.

**19. Does Directo notify when the post is published?** You can track the status on the Dashboard (Draft -\> Scheduled -\> Published).

**20. How do I suggest a new feature?** We love feedback! Use the support button at the bottom of the screen.
