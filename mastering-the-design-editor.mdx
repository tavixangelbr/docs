## 7. Mastering the Design Editor

Our editor is intuitive yet powerful.

### Layers and Tools:

- **Drag and Drop:** Reposition elements freely.
- **AI Assistant in the Editor:** Click on any text and ask the AI to "Improve", "Shorten", or "Add Emojis".
- **Image Swap:** Generate a new AI image directly from within the slide.
