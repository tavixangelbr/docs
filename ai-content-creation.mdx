## 5. AI Content Creation

The heart of Directo. Use AI to generate entire carousels.

### Workflow:

- **Theme:** Type what you want to teach.
- **Image Style:** Choose between Realistic, Illustration, or Minimalist.
- **Structure:** The AI defines the hook, the content, and the CTA (call to action).
